Common Questions

What do I need to get started?

I need: 1. Your Logo (vector preferred), 2. Label/container dimensions, 3. All text (ingredients, descriptions), 4. Barcode (if available).

Do you provide the editable source file?

Yes — you receive the master Adobe Photoshop (PSD) file. I can also export any format you want (PDF, PNG, JPG, EPS, AI on request).

Can you design the logo too?

Yes — logo design is available as an add-on so we can create a cohesive brand system and label together.

How long does a label design take?

Most labels take 1–2 days depending on complexity and revisions.

Can you match my brand style?

Yes. Share brand colors, fonts, or references and I’ll match them so the label feels like part of your brand.

Can you print the labels for me?

I don't handle printing, but I deliver print-ready files and clear printing specs (material, finish, color profile) so your printer can run them without issues.

Do you create packaging mockups?

Yes. I provide high-quality mockups so you can preview the label on the actual product before printing.

What if I don’t have a barcode yet?

No problem — I can leave space for it or advise how to buy a GS1 barcode and integrate it so it prints and scans correctly.

Do you follow industry regulations?

I format labels to common industry standards and can follow specific rules you provide. Final legal/regulatory approval should be done by the client or their compliance officer.

Can you redesign an existing label?

Absolutely — I can refresh or fully rebuild an old label while keeping the elements that already work for you.

What formats do you deliver?

Master PSD file plus any exports you need: PDF, PNG, JPG, EPS — and AI if required. Tell me the printer’s demands and I’ll match them.

Do you offer unlimited revisions?

I include multiple revision rounds; the exact number depends on the package. Premium packages include extended rounds for thorough polishing.

Can you handle multiple product labels at once?

Yes — I can deliver a full line of labels that share consistent branding and structure to speed up printing and approvals.
